Yurin-an

ゲストハウス

We are a guesthouse & cafe "YUURIN-AN" using the old houses built for 100 years. It is the center of Bikan historical quarter at Kurashiki where the familiar townscape of the Edo period still remains, and we have about 60,000 travelers annually.

Our philosophy is "To create a life rich in spirit".

Having a meal good for health Living with the history of the land. To know both the goodness of something old and something new. Cherish those close to you.

We would like to increase such a lifestyle as much as possible.

There are art museums and various shops in Bikan historical quarter at Kurashiki where you can feel the feeling of the people who lives there. We would be greatly appreciated if you could feel the air that mixed atmosphere and culture, which you can only feel here even if you look around the world.
