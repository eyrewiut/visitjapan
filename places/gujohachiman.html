Gujo hachiman

Gifu

Gujo hachiman is a traditional, historic town with much of the town protected as "important preservation buildings", specifically the buildings along Ote machi, machi, Yanagi machi and machi. The Gujo Hachiman railway station, built in 1929, has also been designated as an "Important cultural property" and was carefully restored to it's original design
