Hirosaki Castle

Aomori

Hirosaki park and castle is one of Japan's best spots for viewing the beautiful cherry blossoms from April to May, it has an abundence of over 2500 Sakura trees and a festival is held annually while they are out, the number of sakura petals falling during this time turns the moats around completely pink with Sakura petals. However, he died before initial construction was complete and so it was left to the 9th daimyo; Tsugaru Yasuchika.

The current castle is a 3 story building with 3 roofs and is 14.4 meters high, however, this is smaller than the original building which had 5 stories.

There are a few other interesting places to visit in Hirosaki park; one being the Botanical Garden in the park's southeast corner and Gokoku Shrine as well as the Aomori branch of the Yasukuni Shrine. Many of the city's other tourist attractions are also located within walking distance of the park.
